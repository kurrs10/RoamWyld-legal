Common questions

Is entry requirement information free?

Yes, completely free. Visa rules, passport validity, and entry checklists for 50+ destinations are available to all users with no subscription required. We believe safety-critical travel information should never be paywalled.

Does the app work without internet access?

Yes. Your trips, bookings, entry requirements, currency data, and emergency info are all cached locally on your device. You can access them in airplane mode, on a cruise, or anywhere without service.

How does Gmail import work?

With a Pro subscription, you can connect your Gmail account. Roam Wyld reads your inbox for travel confirmation emails (flights, hotels, Airbnb), extracts the booking details, and lets you add them to your trip in one tap. We only read emails — we never send, delete, or modify anything.

Which countries are supported for entry requirements?

We cover 50+ popular destinations with US, UK, Australian, Canadian, and EU passport data. We're adding more countries continuously. Data is updated regularly but always verify with official government sources before travel.
