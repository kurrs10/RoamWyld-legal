Privacy Policy

Effective May 26, 2026  ·  Last updated May 26, 2026

VOYAGR is a travel organization app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using VOYAGR you agree to the practices described here.

Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

VOYAGR's use of information received from Google APIs (including Gmail) adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. See developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy.

  • Gmail data is used only to find travel confirmation emails related to your saved trips.
  • Gmail data is not used to serve advertisements.
  • Gmail data is not transferred to third parties except as strictly necessary to provide Gmail Import (Anthropic Claude API for parsing, described below).
  • We do not allow humans to read your Gmail messages.
  • Gmail data is not used for any purpose other than the specific Gmail Import feature you activate.

What we collect

Account
Your email address, used only to sign in and identify your data.
Trips & bookings
Trip names, destinations, travel dates, and booking details (flight numbers, hotel names, confirmation codes, activities) that you enter manually or import.
Gmail (optional)
If you use Gmail Import, VOYAGR requests read-only access to your Gmail account to find travel confirmation emails related to your trips. We do not store your Gmail password or credentials. Access can be revoked at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
Usage analytics
Anonymized feature usage events (e.g. which screens you visit) collected via PostHog to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.
Error reports
Anonymized crash and error logs collected via Sentry to help us fix bugs.

What we do NOT collect

  • Payment information of any kind
  • Background location
  • Contacts or call history
  • Gmail content outside of travel-related confirmation emails
  • Full email bodies or attachments (only metadata and snippets are processed)

How we use your data

  • To store and display your trips and bookings within the app
  • When Gmail Import is used, email metadata and snippets are sent to Anthropic's Claude API via our secure backend to extract booking details. This data is not stored by VOYAGR after processing is complete.
  • Analytics events help us understand which features are most useful
  • Error reports help us identify and fix crashes

Third-party services

Supabase
Database and authentication provider. Your trip and account data is stored on Supabase servers. Privacy Policy
Anthropic (Claude)
AI processing for Gmail Import. Email snippets are sent for analysis and are not retained by Anthropic beyond the request. Privacy Policy
Mapbox
Mapping and transit directions. Map interactions may be processed by Mapbox. Privacy Policy
PostHog
Anonymous usage analytics. Privacy Policy
Sentry
Crash and error monitoring. Privacy Policy

Data sharing & sale

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above, solely to operate the app.

Data retention & deletion

Your account and all associated trip data are retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Anonymized analytics and error logs may be retained longer as they cannot be linked back to you.

Your rights

  • Access or update your profile data in the app at any time
  • Revoke Gmail access at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Request deletion of your account and all data by contacting us
  • Contact us with any privacy question or concern

Children

VOYAGR is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will notify you within the app. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
